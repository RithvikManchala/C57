Mahendra Singh Dhoni, is an Indian International Cricket player who captained the Indian National Team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2016 and in test Cricket 2008 to 2014. He is the only Captain in the History of Cricket to win all ICC Trophies. Under his Captaincy Idia won the ICC World T20, 2010 AND 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC World Cup & the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. Aright handed middle ordered Batsman & Wicket Keeper. Dhoni is one of the heighest run scorers in ODIs with more than 10,000 run scored and is considered the effective finisher in the limited-overs formats. He is also regarded by some as one of the best Wicket-Keeper and Captain in modern limited-overs Internatonal Cricket.
